ITA 2.0 · Strategic Vision

ITA 2.0 is not an incremental improvement. It is a foundational replatform — from a standalone advisory calculator to an agentic, AI-first intelligence layer that powers prosperity for every participant across the Intuit ecosystem. A full strategy paper details the architecture; this is the operating summary that frames every use case that follows.

UC-S07P1
QB Capital: True After-Tax Cost of a Business Loan
"What does this loan actually cost me after the interest deduction and depreciation on the equipment I'm buying with it?"
A small business owner is considering a QB Capital loan offer. ITA calculates the effective after-tax cost by factoring in deductible interest and potential Section 179 / bonus depreciation — making it easier to take action.
QB Capital deck SMB conversion driver
Details
How It Works

Most business owners compare loans by face rate alone — missing the tax benefit that makes the effective cost materially lower. ITA uses the client's actual marginal rate from their projection to calculate the real number.

Loan Interest Paid − Tax Deduction Value − Depreciation Benefit = Effective After-Tax Cost
  • Interest Deduction: Business loan interest is fully deductible — at a 28% effective rate, a $10K interest payment costs only $7,200 after tax, calculated using the client's actual marginal rate from the ITA projection
  • Section 179 / Bonus Depreciation: If the loan finances qualifying equipment or assets, the full purchase price may be deductible in Year 1 — dramatically reducing effective cost in the first year
  • Side-by-Side Comparison: "Take this loan" vs. "Don't" — projected net liability, cash flow impact, and break-even horizon
  • Integration: QB Capital loan offer data feeds into the ITA projection; ITA returns the effective cost calculation surfaced at the point of loan decision
Evidence: QB Capital deck: Section 179, Bonus Depreciation, and Buy vs. Lease are core strategies for SMB capital decisions. Most business owners make financing decisions without a tax lens — the gap between nominal and effective cost is often 25–40% of the stated rate. ITA is the only product in the Intuit ecosystem that can calculate this personalized to the client's actual tax situation.
